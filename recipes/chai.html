Chai




Description

Chai is a wonderful way to relax. It is not just the drink, it contains all the healing properties that desi people love so much, from the chapal slaps to the gentle caress of a 100% on a major exam. This dairy-based drink has the caffiene you need to instill passion and fear into your children while maintaining a sense of pride and bliss. A perfect way to start your day and end your life with, you will wish you had drank this drank before!!!!!!!





Ingredients

  • Milk - 1/2 Cup
  • Water - 1/2 Cup
  • Black Royal Tea - 2-3 Bags
  • Sugar - 2 teaspoons [Depends on diabetes]




Materials

  • Saucepan




Instructions

  1. Heat saucepan up to medium-high heat
  2. Add in milk and water (equal amounts or depending on your taste)
  3. Add the tea bags
  4. Stir every now and then (~for around a minute until the drink starts tanning)
  5. Goal is to carefully watch until its boiling
  6. Once boiling or bubbling, add sugar. [BE CAREFUL IT WILL BOIL OVER IF YOU LOOK AWAY!!!]
  7. Turn off heat when dark brown/gold
  8. Check for preferred sugar content
  9. Add cinammon or whatever else. This is extremely basic so just chill
  10. Relax and enjoy

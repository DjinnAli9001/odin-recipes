Omelette

Description

I like eggs. Omelette is peak. MMMMMMMM

Ingredients

  • Eggs - 2-3 Whole
  • Oil - 2 teaspoons
  • Salt and pepper - pinch or less than a pinch of each
  • Onions - 1/8 whole onion: Chopped
  • Bell Peppers - 1/8 whole sliced thinly or chopped
  • Milk - 1 teaspoon OR BUTTER [OPTIONAL]

Materials

  • Bowl
  • Frying Pan

Instructions

  1. Take the bowl, eggs(crack into bowl) with onions, bell peppers, salt and pepper
  2. Add the milk or melted butter for fluffiness
  3. Heat saucepan up to medium heat
  4. Wait until the oil has heated up and has low viscosity
  5. Pour mix onto oil in frying pan
  6. Medium-low heat is best to get even. fuck runny omelettes smfh don't even know how to cook eggs
  7. Watch and flip once you get a feel of the bottom with your spatula. Cut in half if necessary
  8. Should be browning when you need to flip. I like the crispiness but you do whatever idc
  9. Should not be too fluffy otherwise it won't cook all the way through
    • ***Can also literally add any veggies. I love the crunch
